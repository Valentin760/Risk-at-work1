Work Risks — What You Should Know

  • Office / Computer Work
  • Garden / Outdoor Work
  • Construction Site
  • Maintenance / Dangerous
  • Cleaning

Office / Computer Work

Risks:

  • Poor posture → musculoskeletal disorders (back, neck, wrists…)
  • Eye strain, fatigue

Recommendations:

  • Use ergonomic chair, set screen at correct height
  • Take regular breaks, stretch regularly

Garden / Outdoor Work

Possible Risks:

  • Slips, trips, unstable ground
  • Injuries from tools or materials
  • Exposure to weather: sun, rain, heat or cold

Recommendations:

  • Wear proper non-slip shoes, use suitable tools
  • Maintain tools regularly
  • Protect from sun, stay hydrated, wear suitable clothing

Construction Site

Risks:

  • Falls from height, slips
  • Injuries with heavy tools or materials
  • Noise, dust, environmental hazards

Recommendations:

  • Wear helmet, gloves, safety boots, reflective vest
  • Use hearing and eye protection
  • Follow site safety rules and signage

Maintenance / Dangerous

Risks:

  • Accidents from machines
  • Noise, vibrations, dust
  • Chemicals or hazardous atmosphere depending on activity

Recommendations:

  • Wear appropriate protection: helmet, gloves, safety glasses, hearing protection
  • Follow machine safety procedures
  • Ensure proper ventilation, use masks if necessary

Cleaning

Risks:

  • Risk of back injuries
  • Slips if floor is wet or cluttered
  • Contact with chemicals or irritants

Recommendations:

  • Wear gloves, non-slip shoes
  • Ensure floor is safe, clean obstacles
  • Ventilate area if chemicals used
